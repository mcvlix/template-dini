Dini's Surface

A parametric curve defined by the twisting of a pseudosphere. Also the first figure I visualized in WebGL using vertex and fragment shaders.

Its 3D coordinates are with 4 parameters: \(a,b,u,v\)

\[ x = acos(u)sin(v) \] \[ y = asin(u)cos(v) \] \[ z = a\left(cos(v)+lntan(\frac{v}{2})\right) + bu\]

In this animation: \(a\) (spiral width) is animated, \(b\) (vertical spread) is constant, upper limit \(u\) animated synchronously with a, and lower limit \(v\) is constant.
